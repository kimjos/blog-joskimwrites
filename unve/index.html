Unveilling @joskimwrites: Exploring the world of cyber security and love for #ReadingBooks

Joseph Kuria

Jul 5, 2023 • 1 min read

Welcome to my blog, "@joskimwrites: Where the World of Security Meets the Realm of Literature!".
Ha ha ha! I thought that sounded good.

Join me on an exciting journey where I blend my love for cyber security, infosec, and computer security with my passion for reading novels and books.

As someone who finds solace and joy in learning new things related to this field of cyber, unraveling the complexities of cyber security, and diving into the depths of digital protection, this blog is my way of sharing my knowledge and experiences (the little I know) with you.

At @joskimwrites, I aim to showcase my progress in this world of security. I will be writing not according to my experience but my willingness to learn and practice while keeping a record showing how far I have come.

I like reading a lot. Novels, journals, listening to podcasts, music, and most importantly, learning how to write(blogging).

So, grab your favorite cyberpunk novel, equip yourself with knowledge, and join me on this captivating journey. Welcome to @joskimwrites, where we bridge the gap between the digital frontier and the written word!
